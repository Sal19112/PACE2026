📣 Campaign Overview
A three-month performance competition (July – September 2026) across seven area teams, aimed at improving remittance volume and customer acquisition across key corridors.
⚽ The 4 KPIs — Goal Categories
KPI 1
Remittance Target
Growth in overall remittance transaction volume against target. The team with the higher percentage growth wins this KPI.
KPI 2
Customer Registration
Growth in new verified customer registrations against target. Higher percentage growth wins the KPI.
KPI 3
India Corridor
Growth in India-bound remittance transactions. Higher percentage growth wins this KPI.
KPI 4
Egypt Corridor
Growth in Egypt-bound remittance transactions. Higher percentage growth wins this KPI.
Each match compares all 4 KPIs between the two competing teams. For every KPI, the team with the higher percentage growth wins that goal.
🏆 Match Scoring Rules
3
WIN
Team wins more KPI goals than opponent in the match week
1
DRAW
Both teams win an equal number of KPI goals
0
LOSS
Team wins fewer KPI goals than opponent
Winning Criteria
Each of the 4 KPIs is a separate "goal." The team with the higher percentage growth in a KPI wins that goal. The team winning more KPIs wins the overall match (max 4 goals per match).
Tie Handling — Null
If both teams post the same percentage on a KPI, that KPI is marked null and does not count toward the match result.
📅 Format & Structure
Campaign Period 1 July – 30 September 2026
Format Double Round-Robin — each team plays every other team twice (home & away)
Total Match Weeks 14 weeks
Matches Per Week 3 matches — one team rests each week
KPIs Compared All 4 KPIs compared between the two competing teams each match
Total Fixtures 42 matches across the season
Teams 7 area teams, each covering multiple branches

👥 Team Structure
7 area teams, each covering multiple branches. Every team is led by:
Manager
Team ownership, target follow-up and performance direction.
Captain
Daily coordination, motivation and branch follow-up.
Vice Captain
Backup coordination and execution support.
🎁 Bonus Track — Inactive Customer Revival (Win-Back)
Inactive Customer Revival is tracked separately and awarded as bonus league points at month-end, based on each team's revival % (see the points ladder under Rewards). It is not part of the core 4 match KPIs and does not affect individual match results — but it can move the league standings.
